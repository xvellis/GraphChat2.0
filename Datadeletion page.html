Data Deletion Instructions

We respect your privacy and provide you with the ability to request the deletion of your data collected by our application, GraphChat2.0.

How to Request Data Deletion:

To request the deletion of all data associated with your account, please send an email to our support team at:

xvellis@gmail.com

Please use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request".

Once we receive your request, we will begin the process of permanently deleting your data from our systems. This process will be completed within 10 days of your request.

How to Disconnect the App from Facebook:

You can also disconnect our app from your Facebook account at any time. To do this, you can follow the official instructions on Facebook's Help Center, or go directly to your Business Integrations settings page to manage or remove the app.
